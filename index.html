Game Developer

Game Developer

Even though I am a Software Engineer, I tend to develop on the more artistic side of things. I identify mainly as a game developer and have spent the last several years developing interactive experiences in both a competition and hobbyist setting. I believe games are an artform and want to develop games that leave a lasting impact on the player.

Writer

Writer/Musician

I thoroughly believe that being a well-rounded artist leads to being able to create better works in all fields. I've been a musician for over ten years starting with the Clarinet and recently learning piano. Writing is another medium that I enjoy pursuing. From musical compositions to technical papers on game design to long works of fiction, I love being able to share my worlds, thoughts, and analysis on a wide variety of topics

[switch]

Overview

[switch] is a PC Game developed using Game Maker 8.1. It's a dark puzzle-platformer focused on the player altering the solidarity of black and white objects in the stage while telling the dark story of 'Alice'. The project was a collaborative effort done by several of the members of ASU's Software Developer's Association where I served as Project Lead, Programmer, and Level Designer.
